Paid Research Opportunities: These programs usually open late October and close as late as March. Each application usually requires specific prompts, letters of recommendation, and in some cases, U.S. citizenship. If you're not a citizen, let me know, there might be other programs or alternative ways to apply. These opportunities are great since most of them cover travel, housing, education, and even offer a stipend. Even if you're not planning to go into research, they're still worth doing. Since most of them take place over the summer, it's a great way to spend that time. These programs are under looked. Please, consider applying here.
